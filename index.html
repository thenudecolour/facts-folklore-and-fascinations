    Welcome…

    Fáilte…

    Croeso…

    Fair faa ye…

     

    to the United Kingdom!

    The Big Book of the UK introduces readers to British customs and history. Read about the “dwile flonking” sport, and find out where black diamonds originate. Meet the mysterious beast that has plagued the Scottish Highlands and discover the British sweets that helped Everest to scale Hillary and Tenzing.

    The book will help you discover national secrets and unearth local legends from England, Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland, packed with information about nature, food, sports, geography, language, and some very funny names of places.
